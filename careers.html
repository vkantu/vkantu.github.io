Business Data Systems Analyst

Location: Work-at-home (must be within commuting distance of Jefferson Hills, PA for weekly trips)

Overview: RK Technologies Inc. (Jefferson Hills, PA) seeks a Business Data Systems Analyst to manage, analyze, and optimize data operations across 14 Domino’s franchise locations. This role supports leadership with evidence-based reporting, centralized dashboards, automated ETL, statistical and operational analyses, and governance for corporate reporting.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop digital reporting systems to support company leadership in operational decision-making.
  • Design and maintain centralized BI dashboards visualizing sales, labor costs, delivery KPIs, and store performance.
  • Build and manage automated ETL pipelines consolidating daily transactional data from POS, CRM, inventory, and HR systems.
  • Conduct statistical and operational analyses using Excel, Python, and/or R to produce management reports and forecasts.
  • Implement data-validation, governance, and security standards for corporate-level reporting compliance.
  • Collaborate with franchise owners and corporate partners to translate business needs into data models and insights.
  • Identify performance anomalies, recommend process improvements, and support predictive staffing and logistics planning.
  • Maintain documentation of analytics processes and train store managers in data interpretation and reporting usage.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree (or foreign equivalent) in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Systems Management, or a related field.
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in a data analysis role.
  • At least 2 years' experience with each of: business intelligence, KPI reporting, data processing & visualization, statistical analysis, and integrating multi-source business systems in a retail/service environment.
  • Hands-on knowledge (via coursework or real-world experience) of Power BI or Tableau, Excel, SQL, and basic programming for automation.
  • Familiarity with database structures, POS data formats, and performance analytics for retail operations.

Work Arrangement: This is a work-at-home position with required periodic travel to the Jefferson Hills, PA office for weekly meetings.
